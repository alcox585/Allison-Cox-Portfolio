About me

I am currently studying Web Design and design principles at Wake Tech and am living in Wake Forest, NC. I am motivated by my desire to learn new skills and make improvements to the world around me.

Through the classes that I have taken so far at Wake Tech, I have acquired new skills in Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. I have also learned to develope websites and enjoy the process of doing so. I intend to continue learning after I finish school.

In my spare time, I can be found at a local museum, at a park, or in the library. I enjoy other hobbies such as practicing Taekwondo, playing the guitar, crocheting, and photography. If you'd like to see some of my photography work visit my Flickr account. Allison Cox Flickr
